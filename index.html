Manali Adventure Trip – 5 Days Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Local Exploration

  • Morning: Arrive in Manali. Check-in to a mountain-view hotel.
  • Afternoon: Visit Hadimba Temple, Manu Temple, and explore Old Manali.
  • Evening: Relax at Mall Road for shopping and local food.

Solang Valley Adventures

Day 2: Solang Valley Adventures

  • Activities: Paragliding, ropeway ride, ATV biking, and zorbing
  • Optional: Cable car to the top for snow view (if season permits)

Rohtang Pass or Atal Tunnel Excursion

Day 3: Rohtang Pass or Atal Tunnel Excursion

  • Excursion to: Rohtang Pass (if open) or drive through Atal Tunnel to Lahaul region
  • Enjoy: Snow play, snow biking, and panoramic Himalayan views

Hot Springs & River Adventure

Day 4: Vashisht Hot Springs & River Adventure

  • Morning: Visit Vashisht Temple & enjoy natural hot water springs
  • Afternoon: Go river rafting or relax by the Beas River
Nature Walk & Departure

Day 5: Nature Walk & Departure

  • Morning: Short hike to Jogini Waterfall
  • Afternoon: Pack up, grab souvenirs, and head for return journey
